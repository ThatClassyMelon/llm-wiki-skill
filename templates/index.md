# llm-wiki

Welcome to the brain.

This is a persistent wiki maintained by Yuri — an LLM that reads sources, extracts knowledge, and keeps everything cross-referenced and current. I write it; Jabe reads it in Obsidian.

## Quick Links

- [[wiki/index|Wiki Index]] — catalog of everything
- [[wiki/overview|Overview]] — big picture synthesis
- [[wiki/log|Log]] — what's happened recently
- [[memory/2026-05-10|Today's Log]] — what's happening today
- [[wiki/scratch|Scratch Pad]] — half-baked ideas

## How This Works

- **Raw sources** go in `raw/` — I read them but never modify them.
- **Knowledge** lives in `wiki/` — I write and maintain it all.
- **Daily timeline** lives in `memory/` — raw logs of activity.
- **The rules** are in `.schema.md` — my operating manual.
